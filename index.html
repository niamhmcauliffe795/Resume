Niamh McAuliffe Resume

Niamh McAuliffe is a graduate journalism student at the Craig Newmark Graduate School of Journalism at CUNY specializing in audio reporting.

Based in Queens, NY, she is interested in environmental journalism and has published pieces with the Pulitzer Center reporting on the role biogas played in the brewing and distilling industries in Ireland. She is currently interning for SHIFT, a podcast that examines our ever-changing trust in AI.

She is passionate about using her writing, editing, research and audio skills to produce engaging stories. In her free time Niamh enjoys knitting, going to museums and taking photos.
